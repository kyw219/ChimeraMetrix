ChimeraMatrix is an AI-powered video content analysis and performance prediction platform. It analyzes video content using Google Gemini's multimodal AI to extract features (category, emotion, visual style, keywords, audience, hook type), generates optimized content strategies (cover images, titles, hashtags, posting times), and predicts performance based on historical data using semantic matching. The platform supports YouTube, TikTok, and YouTube Shorts, helping content creators optimize their video metadata before publishing.
